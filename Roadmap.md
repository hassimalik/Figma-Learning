# 🎨 30-Day Figma Roadmap
### From Zero to Freelance-Ready — 1 to 1.5 Hours Per Day

> **Rules:** Max 1.5 hrs/day. Watch (20 min) → Practice (40–60 min) → Done. No skipping days.

---

## ⚙️ Before Day 1 — Setup (15 min only)
- Go to [figma.com](https://figma.com) → create a free account
- Watch: *"Figma for Beginners – Interface Tour"* on YouTube (pick any short one)
- That's it. Start Day 1 tomorrow fresh.

---

## 📅 WEEK 1 — Get Comfortable (Basics)

### Day 1 — Interface & Navigation
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** Canvas, toolbar, layers panel, frames vs groups, zoom shortcuts
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Create a simple **business card** — name, title, rectangle background, some color
- **Tip:** Use `F` to create a frame, `T` for text, `R` for rectangle

---

### Day 2 — Shapes & Colors
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** Rectangle, ellipse, fill, stroke, opacity, color picker
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Design a **simple logo** using only shapes — your initials or anything creative
- **Tip:** Hold `Shift` while resizing to keep proportions

---

### Day 3 — Text & Typography
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** Font size, weight, line height, letter spacing, Google Fonts in Figma
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Design an **Instagram quote card** (1080x1080px) with a nice quote and typography
- **Tip:** Limit yourself to 2 fonts max per design

---

### Day 4 — Auto Layout ⭐ (Most Important)
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Learn (30 min):** Auto layout, padding, gap, horizontal vs vertical direction — watch a dedicated video on this
- **Practice (60 min):** 🛠 Design a **button** — then make it stretch properly using auto layout
- **Tip:** `Shift + A` = Add auto layout. Master this and you're ahead of 80% of beginners

---

### Day 5 — Components
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** Main component vs instance, creating and reusing components
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Design a **profile card component** — reuse it 3 times with different names/photos
- **Tip:** `Ctrl + Alt + K` = Create component

---

### Day 6 — Icons & Images
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** Importing images, using Iconify plugin (install it free from Figma Community)
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Design a **mobile app screen** with a header, icons in a grid, and a bottom nav bar
- **Tip:** Install Iconify plugin — it gives you 100k+ free icons

---

### Day 7 — Week 1 Project
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Design a full **Hero Section** of a website
  - Navbar with logo + links
  - Big heading + subtext + CTA button
  - A simple illustration or image
- Use everything from Week 1

---

## 📅 WEEK 2 — UI Design Fundamentals

### Day 8 — Color Theory
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** 60-30-10 color rule, primary/neutral/accent, generating palettes on [coolors.co](https://coolors.co)
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Pick a color palette and apply it to a **pricing card** design
- **Tip:** Don't use more than 3 colors in one design as a beginner

---

### Day 9 — Spacing & Grids
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** 8pt spacing rule, layout grids, columns for desktop/mobile
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Redesign Day 5's profile card using **proper 8pt spacing**
- **Tip:** Add a layout grid (`Ctrl + Shift + 4`) to every frame you design

---

### Day 10 — Mobile UI Design
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** iPhone frame size (390x844), safe zones, mobile UI patterns
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Design a **mobile login screen** — email field, password field, login button, social logins
- **Tip:** Use the built-in iPhone 14 frame in Figma

---

### Day 11 — Desktop UI Design
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** 1440px desktop frame, navbar patterns, desktop spacing differences
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Design a **navbar + hero section** for a SaaS website
- **Tip:** Keep navbar height around 64–80px

---

### Day 12 — Forms & Input Fields
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** Input field design, states (default, focused, error), labels, placeholders
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Design a full **contact form page** with name, email, message, and submit button
- **Tip:** Always design at least 2 states: default + error

---

### Day 13 — Variants (Advanced Components)
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Learn (30 min):** Component variants, property panel, naming conventions
- **Practice (60 min):** 🛠 Build a **button component** with 4 variants — Primary, Secondary, Disabled, Loading
- **Tip:** Name variants clearly: `Type=Primary`, `State=Hover`

---

### Day 14 — Week 2 Project
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Design a **mobile food app home screen**
  - Header with greeting + avatar
  - Search bar
  - Category chips (Burger, Pizza, Sushi...)
  - Featured restaurant cards
  - Bottom navigation bar

---

## 📅 WEEK 3 — Real World Projects

### Day 15 — Dashboard UI
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Design an **admin dashboard homepage**
  - Sidebar with nav links
  - 4 stat cards (Users, Revenue, Orders, etc.)
  - A simple table or list
- **Tip:** Look at [dribbble.com](https://dribbble.com) for dashboard inspiration

---

### Day 16 — E-Commerce UI
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Practice (60 min):** 🛠 Design a **product listing page**
  - Filter sidebar
  - Product cards in a grid (image, name, price, rating, add to cart)
- **Tip:** Use placeholder images from [unsplash.com](https://unsplash.com)

---

### Day 17 — Your Portfolio Design
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Design **your own portfolio homepage** in Figma
  - Hero with your name + role
  - Skills section
  - Projects section
  - Contact CTA
- **Tip:** This is real — you can actually build this later in Next.js

---

### Day 18 — Social Media App UI
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Practice (60 min):** 🛠 Design an **Instagram-style profile screen**
  - Profile photo, name, bio, stats (posts/followers/following)
  - Story circles
  - Posts grid

---

### Day 19 — Full Landing Page
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Design a complete **SaaS landing page**
  - Hero section
  - Features section (3 feature cards)
  - Pricing section
  - Footer
- This is a Fiverr-worthy deliverable

---

### Day 20 — Dark Mode Design
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (15 min):** Dark color palette rules, contrast ratios, what colors work in dark mode
- **Practice (45 min):** 🛠 Redesign Day 10's login screen in **dark mode**
- **Tip:** Dark mode backgrounds: `#121212`, `#1E1E1E`, `#0F0F0F`

---

### Day 21 — Week 3 Project
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Design a **food delivery app — 3 screens**
  1. Home screen
  2. Restaurant menu screen
  3. Cart screen

---

## 📅 WEEK 4 — Professional & Freelance Ready

### Day 22 — Prototyping
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** Linking frames, click interactions, transitions in Figma
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Take Day 21's food app and make it a **clickable prototype**
- **Tip:** Use the Prototype tab → drag connections between frames

---

### Day 23 — Design System Basics
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Learn (30 min):** What is a design system, color tokens, typography scale, component library
- **Practice (60 min):** 🛠 Build a **mini design system file**
  - Color palette (primary, neutrals, semantic)
  - Typography scale (H1–H6, body, caption)
  - Button, input, card components

---

### Day 24 — Developer Handoff
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (20 min):** Inspect panel, how devs read spacing/CSS from Figma, exporting assets
- **Practice (40 min):** 🛠 Take any past design → properly **name all layers**, set up **export settings**, and share the inspect link
- **Tip:** This is a skill that will impress clients and employers

---

### Day 25 — Thumbnail Design
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Practice (60 min):** 🛠 Design a **YouTube thumbnail**
  - 1280x720px frame
  - Bold text, face image or illustration, bright background
- **Tip:** Use contrast — dark text on light bg or vice versa

---

### Day 26 — Social Media Templates
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Design **3 Instagram templates**
  1. Square post (1080x1080)
  2. Story (1080x1920)
  3. Horizontal banner (1200x628)

---

### Day 27 — Redesign Challenge
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Pick any app you use daily (YouTube, WhatsApp, Twitter) and **redesign 1 screen** your way — make it better
- **Tip:** This is one of the best portfolio pieces you can have

---

### Day 28 — Mockup Presentation
**⏱ Time:** 1 hour
- **Learn (15 min):** Using device mockups in Figma (search "Mockup" in Community files)
- **Practice (45 min):** 🛠 Place your best 2–3 designs inside **phone and laptop mockups** — presentation ready
- **Tip:** Mockup shots make your Fiverr gig look 10x more professional

---

### Day 29 — Build Your Portfolio File
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Create a **Figma Portfolio Presentation**
  - Pick your 5 best projects
  - Add a cover page with your name + role
  - One slide per project with mockup + short description
  - This is what you send to clients and employers

---

### Day 30 — Final Capstone Project 🏆
**⏱ Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Practice (90 min):** 🛠 Design a **complete app or website from scratch** — your choice
  - Minimum 5 screens/sections
  - Proper color system
  - Components used throughout
  - Prototype connected
  - Developer handoff ready
  - Dark mode (bonus)
- **This goes straight into your Fiverr portfolio**

---

## 🧠 Golden Rules for This Roadmap

| Rule | Why |
|---|---|
| Max 1.5 hrs/day | Consistency > long sessions |
| Always do the project | Watching without doing = zero progress |
| Copy real apps first | Fastest way to train your eye |
| Use Figma Community | Free inspiration and UI kits |
| Name your layers | Professional habit from day 1 |
